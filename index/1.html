ICYMI | Talaria Publications' First Club Day

Novemeber 22, 2023

Photos by Lyslie Patawaran

Talaria Publications held their first Club Day on November 15, at the Talaria Publications Headquarters from 12:30 PM to 4:00 PM. They prepared fun, exciting, and nerve-wracking mini-games and snacks for the members to enjoy for the afternoon.
