Oath taking Ceremony of Newly Elected SHSC Officers

Novemeber 09, 2023

Photo by Yuan Domantay

LOOK | An oath-taking ceremony was held on November 9, 2023, at 9:00 a.m. in front of the Office of the President on the 2nd floor of Manila Tytana Colleges, wherein the newly elected Senior High School Student Council (SHS-SC) gathered to formally seal their duties and responsibilities as the official SHS-SC for the academic year 2023-2024.

The ceremony was initiated by the introduction of Ms. Abigail Batuto, the SHS-SC adviser. This was followed by an introduction to the newly elected officers given by Prof. Ofelia Mallabo-Mutas, the Senior High School Department (SHS) Principal and Dean of the College of Arts and Sciences (CAS). Afterward, the oath-taking commenced, led by Dr. Sergio S. Cao, the President and Chief Academic Officer of Manila Tytana Colleges, after his encouraging message for the council. Ms. Anavia Theresa H. Chua, Assistant Vice President and Director of the Student Affairs and Services Directorate; Ms. Anna Marie See, SHS Academic Group Head; Mr. Arnold Paul Angus, Student Activities and Services Head; and Mr. Paolo T. Villanueva, Socio-Cultural and Communications Coordinator, witnessed this momentous ceremony.
