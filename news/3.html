SCAM AND FAKE NEWS DETECTION: Spotting fake news and scam schemes through text, social media, and money related applications

Photos by Chloe Caval, Mery Lamajan, Giane Capili, and Lyslie Patawaran

ICYMI | Last Friday, November 17, 2023, the Manila Tytana Colleges’ Marketing and Public Relations Office (MPRO) conducted a series of seminars titled “WORK-LIFE BALANCE FOR PARENTS: Strategies to maintain a healthy work-life balance while raising adolescent children,” with resource speaker Dr. Lizelle E. Villanueva, Assistant Vice President and Director for Human Capital Management and Professional Services Directorate; and “SCAM AND FAKE NEWS DETECTION: Spotting fake news and scam schemes through text, social media, and money related applications," with resource speaker Prof. Lilio T. Carreon Jr. from the College of Arts and Sciences. The seminars were held at Multi-Purpose Hall A and Lecture Hall B, respectively, from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. for the parents and guardians of Senior High School (SHS) students before proceeding to the respective classrooms for the 1st Quarter card-giving.
