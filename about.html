TALARIA PUBLICATIONS

THE OFFICIAL SCHOOL PUBLICATION OF MANILA TYTANA COLLEGES - SENIOR HIGH DEPARTMENT











Talaria Publications is the Official Student Publication of Manila Tytana Colleges - Senior High School Department. This
Publications serves for the Senior High students of Manila Tytana Colleges in posting news, editorials, and updates
inside and outside the campus. Talaria Publications is part of the College Editors Guild of the Philippines, the oldest,
broadest, and only-existing alliance of tertiary student publications in the Asia Pacific region.
